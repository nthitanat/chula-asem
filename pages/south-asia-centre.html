Our Mission & Vision

The ASEM LLL Hub Regional Centre for South Asia has been established since December 2022 during the Honourable Chair SÃ©amus O' Tuama's mission to promote Lifelong Learning in South Asia.

South Asia does not lack lifelong learning in general and has strong traditional education systems, but lacks dedicated policies on lifelong learning and commitment to resources for the same. Systematic efforts to support the transition of traditional educational systems towards lifelong learning ecosystems can create significant value and inclusiveness in the region by recognising and valuing learning that occurs outside the traditional education systems.

The ASEM LLL Hub Regional Centre for South Asia has a major role to provide inputs and support stakeholders for developing lifelong learning ecosystems in the region. Starting from creating awareness about lifelong learning in South Asia, it now acts as a common platform bringing various stakeholders together for the promotion of lifelong learning in diverse ways.

This type of initiative is unprecedented in South Asia and gradually, stakeholders like educational institutions, civil society organisations, industry, philanthropists, and even state bodies are now partnering with the ASEM LLL Hub Regional Centre for South Asia.

Areas for Collaboration

To develop lifelong learning ecosystems in the region, ASEM LLL Hub Regional Centre for South Asia engages stakeholders along four primary areas:

Internships, Courses & Exchange

Facilitating educational exchanges, internship opportunities, and specialized courses to enhance lifelong learning experiences across South Asia.

Education Exchange Programs Professional Development

Collaborative Projects & Research

Fostering collaborative research initiatives and joint projects that advance lifelong learning methodologies and practices in the region.
