We would like to thank all those who participated across the week for their support and contributions. The full press release on the event is below:

Press Release

May 2024

International conference in Cork to address threats from AI, Misinformation, and Climate

  • Over 50 speakers and 200 participants from over 40 countries to attend Cork conference
  • Global Lifelong Learning Week 2024 addresses Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity, Ambiguity in our world
  • UCC hosting week-long events featuring international educational leaders

The opportunities and threats posed by AI, the ongoing problem of misinformation and fake news, and the ever-present challenge posed by climate change are all topics due to be discussed at this major international conference.

University College Cork (UCC) hosted over 50 speakers and 200 participants from over 40 countries at Global Lifelong Learning Week 2024, which contemplated responses to a so-called "VUCA (Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity, Ambiguity) world".

Key Discussion Topics

Artificial Intelligence

Analyzing jobs likely to be displaced by AI and identifying skills gaps that can be used to upskill displaced employees.

Misinformation & Fake News

Developing critical thinking and analytical skills necessary to analyze information in our digital age.

Climate Change

Addressing environmental challenges through education and sustainable learning practices.

"Lifelong learning is the essential paradigm through which humanity can address the huge global challenges we face today. As a species we have to learn how to adjust to new realities and collectively plot a planetary way forward."

Prof. SÃ©amus O'Tuama, Director of UCC's Adult Continuing Education (ACE)

The week included the ASEM LLL Hub Conference (May 27-29) and the EUCEN Conference (May 29-31) and saw representatives from UNESCO, the European Commission, the Department of Foreign Affairs and the South East Asian Ministers for Education Organisation in attendance.

Event Theme

"University Lifelong Learning: Leading Positive Change in Challenging Times"

This theme highlights the crucial role of continuous education in societal resilience and adaptability.
