Research Networks

Fostering collaboration between Asia and Europe in lifelong learning research through seven specialized networks

ASEM Education and Research Hub

The ASEM Education and Research Hub for Lifelong Learning is an official network for university cooperation in Lifelong Learning Research between Asia and Europe. The Hub creates a platform for dialogue between researchers and educational policy makers through our seven specialized research networks.

Network 1: E-learning

Development of ICT skills, e-learning and the culture of e-learning in Lifelong Learning. This network focuses on digital transformation in education, exploring how technology can enhance learning experiences and outcomes across different cultural contexts.

Digital Skills Online Learning Educational Technology
Learn More

Network 2: Workplace Learning

Decoding working places as lifelong learning spaces across Asia and Europe: the CODE initiative. Investigating how workplaces serve as learning environments and developing strategies to maximize professional development opportunities.

Professional Development Work-based Learning Skills Development
Learn More

Network 3: Professionalisation

Professionalisation of Adult Teachers and Educators in ASEM Countries. Focusing on the development of professional standards, qualifications, and competencies for adult education practitioners across Asia and Europe.

Teacher Training Professional Standards Adult Education
Learn More

Network 4: National LLL Strategies

National lifelong learning strategies with regard to citizens' motivation and barriers to continuing education and training. Examining policy frameworks and strategies that promote lifelong learning at the national level.

Policy Development National Frameworks Learning Motivation
Learn More

Network 5: Learning Transitions

Focus on transitions as they apply to lifelong learning. Exploring the critical moments of change in individuals' learning journeys and how to support successful transitions between different life stages and learning contexts.

Life Transitions Career Changes Learning Pathways
Learn More

Network 6: Learning Cities & Regions

Building a strong research base that spans the many dimensions of Learning Cities & Regions. Investigating how urban and regional environments can be transformed into comprehensive learning ecosystems that benefit all residents.

Urban Learning Community Development Regional Networks
Learn More

Network 7: Non-formal & Informal Learning

Focus on non-formal and informal learning as a way to provide educational opportunities and foster community development. Exploring learning that happens outside traditional educational institutions and its role in personal and community growth.

Community Learning Civil Society Informal Education
